📍 Assignment 1 · The presentation
✅ What I did
Created a basic to-do list. Placed difficult tasks (reading, research) at the top and simpler ones (slide design) at the bottom. Completed the difficult task first — built momentum and reduced procrastination.
📸 EVIDENCE / ARTEFACT
🌟 SKILL GAINED
“Everything else seems easier when difficult tasks are completed first (Häfner, Oberst, and Stock, 2014). Tackling challenging work early creates achievement momentum.”
📅 Häfner, Oberst & Stock, 2014 — avoidance-reduction & sequencing
